No. By Taleb's own definition, **COVID-19 was a White Swan, not a Black Swan.**

Taleb's Black Swan has three properties:
1. **Rarity** (outlier relative to expectations) — ✗ *Fails. Pandemics were explicitly warned about; only the timing was unknown.*
2. **Extreme impact** — ✓ *Clear.*
3. **Retrospective predictability** (we invent explanations after the fact) — ✓ *Yes, but the narrative "nobody could have seen this coming" is itself a classic hindsight-smoothing operation.*

Since property 1 fails, the event cannot be a Black Swan. Taleb has written and stated this position directly.
